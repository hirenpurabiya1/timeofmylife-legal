Privacy Policy - Time of My Life

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Time of My Life ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Account information (name, email)
  • Dreams and goals you set
  • Daily check-in responses
  • Routine preferences

2.2 Data We Access (With Your Permission)

  • Screen Time Data: App usage, pickups, notifications (via Apple Family Controls)
  • Health Data: Sleep, heart rate, HRV, steps, workouts (via Apple HealthKit)
  • Oura Ring Data: Readiness score, sleep score, HRV baseline (via Oura API, optional)

2.3 Data We Calculate

  • Overstimulation Index (OSI)
  • Presence Minutes
  • Productivity correlations
  • Health insights

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized OSI (Overstimulation Index) scores
  • Show health correlations and insights
  • Track your progress toward goals
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Provide AI coaching recommendations

We do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising
  • ❌ Share your data without your consent
  • ❌ Store sensitive data on external servers (data stays on your device)

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Data transmission is encrypted
  • Backup: Optional cloud backup (you control this)
  • Access: Only you can access your data

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple HealthKit

  • We access health data only with your explicit permission
  • You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings
  • We follow Apple's HealthKit privacy guidelines

5.2 Oura Ring (Optional)

  • Oura integration is completely optional
  • You can disconnect anytime
  • We access only data you authorize
  • Oura's privacy policy applies to their data: https://ouraring.com/privacy-policy

5.3 OpenAI (AI Coach)

  • Your conversations are sent to OpenAI for AI responses
  • OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • We don't store your conversations permanently

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✅ Access your data
  • ✅ Delete your data
  • ✅ Disconnect third-party integrations (HealthKit, Oura)
  • ✅ Opt out of data collection
  • ✅ Export your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We'll notify you of significant changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. All data will be permanently deleted
