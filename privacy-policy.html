Privacy Policy – Time of My Life

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Time of My Life ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined here.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name and email (for account creation)
  • Optional self-reported wellness inputs
  • App preferences and settings

2.2 Data Accessed With Your Permission

We access certain data only after you grant explicit permission:

Screen Time & Device Usage Data

  • App usage duration
  • Phone pickups
  • Notifications

(Collected using Apple Family Controls APIs where available)

Apple Health Data (Optional)

  • Heart rate
  • HRV (Heart Rate Variability)
  • Sleep analysis
  • Steps, movement, workouts
  • Activity summaries

Access is permission-based and fully controlled by you.

Oura Ring Data (Optional)

  • Readiness score
  • Sleep score
  • HRV baseline
  • Activity metrics

We only access data you authorize through Oura's OAuth permissions.

2.3 Data We Generate

We generate personalized wellness insights and behavioral analytics to help you understand your digital habits, health patterns, and daily rhythms.

We do not share these insights with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized insights and analytics
  • Improve app experience and features
  • Deliver optional AI-powered recommendations
  • Support integrations (HealthKit, Oura, AI services)

We Do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data
  • ❌ Use health or Oura data for marketing or advertising
  • ❌ Use health or Oura data to train machine learning models
  • ❌ Share your data with third parties without your permission

Health-related data is used only to provide features inside the App.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission
  • Health and Oura data is stored only as needed for app functionality and never used for ads or tracking
  • You control whether you enable cloud backup
  • You can revoke any permission at any time

Data security practices comply with:

  • Apple HealthKit rules
  • Oura API terms
  • App Store Review Guidelines
  • Industry security guidelines

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple HealthKit

We comply with all HealthKit privacy guidelines:

  • Health data is not shared with third parties
  • Health data is not used for advertising or marketing
  • Health data is not used for data mining or LLM training

5.2 Oura Integration (Optional)

  • Access is optional
  • You control permissions
  • Oura data is never shared externally
  • Oura data is not used for advertising or model training

Oura's privacy policy applies to their data: https://ouraring.com/privacy-policy

5.3 AI Services (Optional)

When you use the AI Coaching feature, your message may be sent to a secure third-party AI provider (e.g., OpenAI) to generate a response.

  • We do not store AI conversations long-term unless you choose to save them
  • We do not use your data to train AI models
  • OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • ✅ Access your data
  • ✅ Delete your data
  • ✅ Revoke third-party permissions
  • ✅ Export your data
  • ✅ Opt out of optional analytics
  • ✅ Delete your account at any time

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under age 13.

We do not knowingly collect children's data.

8. Policy Updates

Material changes will be communicated within the App.

Continued use indicates acceptance.
